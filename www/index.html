Day R Survival Trade Router

This tool helps you plan your survivor camp trade routes in the Day R Survival game (v.574).

Instructions:

  • Pick "Want" to see possible routes to get the specified amount of items
  • Pick "Have" to see possible routes starting from the specified amount of items
  • Each indent shows the next chain in the trade route
  • Trades at the same indent mean that they are alternative trade options for the same item
  • Note that when you pick "Want", the item you want to trade for will end up at the bottom
