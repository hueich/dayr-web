Day R Survival Trade Router

This interactive tool helps you plan trade routes to survivors' bases in the Day R Survival game ().

Instructions:

  • Pick "Want" to see possible routes to get the specified amount of items
  • Pick "Have" to see possible routes starting from the specified amount of items
  • Each indent shows the next chain in the route
  • Trades at the same indent mean that they are alternative trade options for the same item
  • Note: when you pick "Want", the item you want is at the top, and the routes are shown in reverse order
  • Allowing fractional trade may show you a fraction of a trade, e.g. 0.5 Vodka to 10 Cigarettes
